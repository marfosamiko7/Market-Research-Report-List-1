<p><strong>The Fixed Inclinometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Dual Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed inclinometer market encompasses two main types: single axis and dual axis inclinometers. Single axis inclinometers measure tilt or inclination along one direction, making them suitable for applications such as slope monitoring and structural assessment. In contrast, dual axis inclinometers can detect tilt in two perpendicular directions, providing more comprehensive data for applications like geotechnical monitoring and construction site stability. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and accuracy in various engineering and environmental projects.</p></p>
