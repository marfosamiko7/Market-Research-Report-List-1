<p><ul><li>Ferrite Core</li><li>Powder Core</li><li>Amorphous Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft magnetic core market comprises various types, including ferrite cores, powder cores, and amorphous cores. Ferrite cores, made of ceramic materials, are widely used in high-frequency applications due to their low eddy current losses. Powder cores consist of magnetic metal powders compacted and insulated, offering flexibility in design and efficient performance in inductors and transformers. Amorphous cores, made from non-crystalline materials, provide excellent magnetic properties and reduced energy losses, making them suitable for high-efficiency power conversion systems.</p></p>
