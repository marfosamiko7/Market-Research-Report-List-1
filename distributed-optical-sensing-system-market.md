<p><p>The Distributed Optical Sensing System market includes three primary types. Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) utilizes optical fibers to monitor temperature along extensive lengths, ideal for pipelines and infrastructure. Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) captures sound waves over distances, facilitating security and monitoring applications. Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS) measures vibrations to detect structural anomalies or assess machinery health. Together, these technologies leverage optical fibers to provide real-time, precise data for various industries, enhancing safety and efficiency in monitoring critical assets.</p></p>
