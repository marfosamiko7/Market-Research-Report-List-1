<p><p>The Personal Emergency Response System (PERS) market is divided into three main types: Landline PERS, Mobile PERS, and Standalone PERS. </p><p>Landline PERS relies on a traditional telephone connection to facilitate emergency communication, making it suitable for users who have a stable landline. Mobile PERS offers cellular connectivity, enabling users to seek help anywhere, ideal for active seniors. Standalone PERS operates independently without the need for external devices or connections, typically featuring built-in alerts, making it simple and user-friendly for immediate assistance.</p></p>
