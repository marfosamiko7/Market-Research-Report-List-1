<p><p>Air stripping equipment is essential for removing volatile organic compounds from water through techniques such as the Sieve Tray System and Packed Tower System. The Sieve Tray System uses perforated trays to facilitate gas-liquid contact, promoting efficient mass transfer. In contrast, the Packed Tower System employs structured packing materials to enhance surface area and improve contact efficiency between air and liquid. Both systems are crucial for optimizing air stripping processes in water treatment, catering to various industrial and environmental needs.</p></p>
