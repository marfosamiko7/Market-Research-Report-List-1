<p><p>Isolated current sensors are vital in measuring electrical currents while ensuring safety by separating the sensor circuitry from high voltage systems. The market is categorized into open-loop and closed-loop types. Open-loop sensors use transformers or Hall-effect devices to provide a simple and inexpensive solution for current measurement. In contrast, closed-loop sensors employ feedback mechanisms to achieve higher precision and accuracy, often used in applications requiring strict performance standards, such as industrial automation and power systems.</p></p>
