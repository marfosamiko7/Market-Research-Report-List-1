<p><p>The Automated Molecular Diagnostics Testing System market includes several key technologies. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences for analysis, crucial in infectious disease detection. FISH (Fluorescent In-situ Hybridization) visualizes specific DNA sequences using fluorescent probes, aiding in cancer diagnostics. Spectral Karyotype Imaging analyzes chromosomal abnormalities through color-coded imagery, enhancing genetic research. DNA Microarrays allow simultaneous analysis of numerous genes, facilitating genomic studies and personalized medicine. Collectively, these technologies drive advancements in rapid, accurate diagnostic solutions.</p></p>
