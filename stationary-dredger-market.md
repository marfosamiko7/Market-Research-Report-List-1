<p><p>The stationary dredger market encompasses various types of equipment used for underwater excavation. Cutter suction dredgers utilize a rotating cutter to loosen material, then suction it away. Backhoe dredgers employ a mechanical arm with a bucket to scoop sediment. Bucket chain dredgers feature a continuous chain of buckets that collects material. Grab dredgers use a clamshell-like mechanism to grasp and lift sediments. The "Other" category includes specialized dredgers designed for unique applications or environments, diversifying the market.</p></p>
