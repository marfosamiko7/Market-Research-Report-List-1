<p><p>The Dynamic Blood Pressure Monitor market comprises two primary types: Ordinary Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitors (ABPM) and Mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM devices are traditional monitors that record blood pressure at regular intervals over 24 hours while the patient goes about daily activities, providing a comprehensive profile of blood pressure changes. In contrast, Mobile-based ABPM integrates smartphone technology, allowing for real-time monitoring and data sharing, enhancing patient engagement and accessibility to healthcare providers for improved management of hypertension.</p></p>
