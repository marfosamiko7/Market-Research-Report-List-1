<p><strong>The High Grade Refractory Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-grade refractory market is categorized into two main types: shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are pre-formed products, such as bricks and tiles, designed for specific applications in high-temperature environments. They offer structural integrity and precise configuration. Unshaped refractories, on the other hand, consist of materials like castables, ramming masses, and mortars, which are mixed and applied directly to surfaces. Both types are crucial for industries like metallurgy, cement, and glass, ensuring thermal stability and resistance to corrosive conditions.</p></p>
