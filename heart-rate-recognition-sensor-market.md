<p><p>The heart rate recognition sensor market is divided into two main types: chest heart rate recognition sensors and wrist heart rate recognition sensors. Chest sensors, typically worn by athletes, provide accurate heart rate measurements using electrodes that detect electrical signals of the heartbeat. They are ideal for rigorous training sessions. In contrast, wrist sensors, found in fitness trackers and smartwatches, offer convenience and continuous monitoring. While they may be slightly less accurate than chest sensors, they appeal to a broader audience due to their ease of use.</p></p>
