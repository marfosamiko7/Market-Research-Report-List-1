<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Through Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zero Phase Current Transformer (ZCT) market is primarily segmented into two types: Split-Type and Through-Type. Split-Type ZCTs are designed with a hinge mechanism that allows the core to be separated for easy installation around existing conductors. In contrast, Through-Type ZCTs consist of a solid core where conductors pass directly through, making them suitable for permanent installation. Both types are essential for protecting electrical systems by detecting imbalances in current flow and ensuring effective fault protection and monitoring.</p></p>
