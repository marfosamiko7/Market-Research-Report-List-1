<p><p>The concrete formwork market comprises several types, each serving specific construction needs. System column formwork is designed for creating vertical structural elements efficiently. Horizontal formwork is used for slabs and beams, ensuring a smooth finish and quick installation. Slipform is a continuous process for vertical structures, allowing construction to progress rapidly without interruptions. Vertical formwork focuses on forming walls and other upright elements, providing versatility and strength. Each type enhances productivity and quality in concrete construction projects.</p></p>
