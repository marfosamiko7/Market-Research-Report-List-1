<p><p>The aviation seatbelt market is categorized into several types based on design and functionality. Two-point seatbelts provide basic restraint with two attachment points, primarily suitable for light aircraft. Three-point seatbelts, which feature an additional anchor point, enhance safety and comfort and are widely used in commercial aircraft. Four-point seatbelts offer superior containment by securing passengers at four points, often used in specialized or high-performance aircraft. Additionally, other types may include advanced restraint systems and harnesses, catering to diverse aviation needs.</p></p>
