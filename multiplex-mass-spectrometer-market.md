<p><strong>The Multiplex Mass Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Riple Quadrupole (Tandem)</li><li>Quadrupole TOF (Q-TOF)</li><li>FTMS (Fourier Transform Mass Spectrometry)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multiplex Mass Spectrometer market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Triple Quadrupole (Tandem)**: Utilizes three quadrupoles for increased sensitivity and specificity, ideal for quantitative analysis. </p><p>2. **Quadrupole Time-of-Flight (Q-TOF)**: Combines quadrupole stability with TOF's high-resolution capabilities, enhancing accurate mass measurements. </p><p>3. **Fourier Transform Mass Spectrometry (FTMS)**: Offers ultra-high-resolution and mass accuracy by measuring ion frequency, making it suitable for complex mixtures. Each type serves distinct analytical needs across various industries, including pharmaceuticals and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
