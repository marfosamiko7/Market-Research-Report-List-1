<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-stage corrosion-resistant magnetic pump market consists primarily of two types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft pumps. Stationary shaft pumps feature a fixed shaft arrangement, with the motor and impeller connected magnetically, allowing for minimal wear and maintenance. In contrast, rotating shaft pumps have an actively rotating shaft that engages directly with the impeller, which can enhance efficiency in certain applications. Both types are designed for handling corrosive fluids, making them essential for industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
