<p><ul><li>Swinging-bucket Rotors</li><li>Fixed-angle Rotors</li><li>Vertical Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical centrifuge market includes three primary rotor types: swinging-bucket rotors, fixed-angle rotors, and vertical rotors. Swinging-bucket rotors allow samples to move outward during rotation, optimizing sample separation and facilitating horizontal orientation. Fixed-angle rotors hold samples at a constant angle, enhancing sedimentation efficiency for denser materials. Vertical rotors position samples vertically, ideal for separating lighter particles or small volumes. Each rotor type serves specific application needs in laboratories and industrial processes, contributing to the overall efficiency of separation techniques.</p></p>
