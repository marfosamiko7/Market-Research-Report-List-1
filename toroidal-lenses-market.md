<p><p>The toroidal lenses market features various types, each serving specific optical applications. Plano-convex toroidal lenses have one flat surface and a convex toroidal shape, ideal for focusing light efficiently. Plano-concave toroidal lenses, with a flat surface and a concave toroidal shape, are used for diverging light beams. Plano-convex-concave toroidal lenses combine both convex and concave surfaces, offering versatile optical properties for specialized uses in imaging and beam shaping, catering to diverse industries such as telecommunications and laser technology.</p></p>
