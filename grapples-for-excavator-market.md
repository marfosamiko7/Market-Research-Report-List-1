<p><p>Grapples for excavators are specialized attachments designed for handling materials like wood, debris, or scrap. The market is divided into two primary types: hydraulic and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize hydraulic power to effectively open and close their jaws, providing precise control and strong gripping capability, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on a simpler mechanical design, typically using a lever system, offering a cost-effective solution for lighter applications and less complex operations.</p></p>
