<p><strong>The Air Hydraulic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Air Hydraulic Pump</li><li>Double Acting Air Hydraulic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air hydraulic pump market encompasses two primary types: single acting and double acting pumps. Single acting air hydraulic pumps use compressed air to move the piston in one direction, typically utilizing a spring for return, making them suitable for applications needing less complexity and lower costs. In contrast, double acting air hydraulic pumps utilize compressed air for both the forward and return strokes, offering higher efficiency and faster operation, ideal for demanding industrial applications requiring continuous hydraulic power.</p></p>
