<p><p>The graphics processors market is primarily divided into two types: discrete and integrated. Discrete graphics processors are standalone units designed for high-performance tasks, such as gaming and professional graphic design, providing superior processing power and memory. They are often found in gaming PCs and workstations. In contrast, integrated graphics processors are built into the CPU, sharing system memory, and are ideal for everyday computing tasks with lower power consumption. Each type serves specific user needs, balancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
