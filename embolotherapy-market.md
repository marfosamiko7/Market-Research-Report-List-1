<p><p>Embolotherapy is a minimally invasive medical procedure used to obstruct blood flow in specific vessels or tumors, predominantly for the treatment of various conditions such as vascular malformations, uterine fibroids, and certain cancers. It involves the injection of embolic agents, such as microspheres, coils, or liquids, into blood vessels to induce embolization, effectively depriving tissues of their blood supply.</p><p>The Embolotherapy Market is on an upward trajectory, anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 9.10% during the forecast period.
